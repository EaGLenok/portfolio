Countering Forest Fires

To stop forest fires, we need to clean the forest of dead trees and plants often. Making clear areas without trees can help stop fires from spreading. Teaching people not to leave fires or throw away cigarettes in the forest is important. Also, having a system to find fires early and a team ready to put out fires fast is necessary.

Global warming

Now i am going to talk about global warming. Global warming is the long-term heating of Earth’s surface due to human activities. It’s a primarily fossil fuel burning. How are we going to solve global warming? We are going to save energy at home, you can walk, bike or take public transport also we can reduce, re-use, repair and recycle.

Water scarcity

The last topic is about water scarcity. Water scarcity is defined as a water deficiency or a lack of safe water supplies. How are we going to solve that? Take shorter showers ,Turn off the water while brushing your teeth ,Turn off the water while shaving.
